12-Light Wagon Wheel Chandelier with Matte Black Finish

This striking 12-light wagon wheel chandelier combines rustic charm with contemporary design to create a timeless piece for your home. Its large, black round metal frame is supported by four sleek matte black linked bars, adding a sense of grandeur and industrial appeal. The exposed bulbs not only offer a warm, vintage glow but also provide a modern touch, making it an excellent choice for both retro and modern farmhouse decor.

Key Features

  • 12 exposed lights for vibrant illumination
  • Matte black finish with a sturdy metal frame
  • Adjustable height with 70-inch chain for versatile installation
  • Easy to assemble with pre-wired components
  • Dimmable with E12 bulbs for customizable lighting

Where to Place It

Perfectly suited for living rooms, dining rooms, foyers, or entryways, this chandelier is designed to complement a variety of ceiling types, including flat, sloped, and vaulted. The adjustable chain ensures you can easily customize the height of the fixture to match your space and personal style.

Why Choose This Chandelier?

With its combination of style and functionality, this chandelier is a versatile lighting solution that can transform the ambiance of any room. Whether you're hosting a dinner party, curling up with a book, or simply enjoying the space, the dimmable feature allows you to adjust the brightness for the perfect setting.
