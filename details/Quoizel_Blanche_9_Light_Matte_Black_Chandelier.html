Blanche 9-Light Chandelier

The Blanche 9-light chandelier brings a bold and sophisticated look to your home, blending striking matte black finishes with a modern, industrial style that seamlessly integrates into any interior. Its exposed bulb design adds a touch of raw charm and personality, making it a standout feature in contemporary or minimalist spaces. Perfect for dining rooms, entryways, or living areas, this chandelier creates a warm, ambient glow while maintaining a high level of design elegance.

With an adjustable height mechanism, the Blanche chandelier offers flexible installation to suit your home's unique layout, whether you have high ceilings or a more compact space. Crafted from durable steel, this chandelier is built to last, ensuring long-term use and a continuous impact on your home's ambiance.
