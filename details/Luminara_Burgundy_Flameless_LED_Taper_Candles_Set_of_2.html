Luminara Burgundy Flameless LED Taper Candles

Transform your holiday decor with these elegant and safe flameless LED taper candles. Designed to simulate the soft, realistic drip of real wax, they bring an authentic touch to your celebration without the dangers of open flame. The warm, flickering glow enhances the cozy and inviting vibe of your living space, making them perfect for creating a festive atmosphere during holidays or special events.

Ideal for mantels, dining tables, and holiday trees, these candles are a stylish addition to any setting. Each candle features a timer function, lighting up for 5 hours and then turning off for 19, for a total of 200 hours of combined use. Powered by AA batteries (sold separately), they offer long-lasting enjoyment and reliable performance. Whether it's Christmas, Halloween, or another holiday, these flameless LED candles deliver both beauty and safety to your home.
