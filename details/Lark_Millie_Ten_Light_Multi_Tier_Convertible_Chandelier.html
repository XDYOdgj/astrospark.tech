Lark Millie Chandelier

Elevate your space with the Lark Millie Chandelier, where timeless design meets practical functionality. This midcentury modern chandelier features five vertical candle sockets, offering a classic yet contemporary lighting solution.

Stylish Design for Every Room

With its geometric silhouette and elegant midcentury modern appeal, the Lark Millie Chandelier blends seamlessly into both traditional and modern interiors. The availability of lacquered brass or black finishes ensures it complements a wide range of design aesthetics, from chic urban spaces to refined country homes.

Versatile Installation Options

Enjoy the flexibility of this convertible chandelier, which can be transformed from a multi-tiered design to a semi-flush mount with ease. It includes 6" and 12" down rods, making it perfect for flat or sloped ceilings. Whether installed in a dining room, foyer, or kitchen, it adapts flawlessly to your space.

Thoughtful Craftsmanship

Each piece is crafted with precision, featuring a modular design that offers convenience and customization. The adjustable down rods make installation simple, while its compact structure ensures it remains a striking focal point without overwhelming your room.

Safe and Reliable

Designed with safety in mind, this chandelier meets UL, CSA, NEC, and CEC standards, ensuring long-lasting performance and worry-free use. The energy-efficient LED bulbs provide bright, clear lighting while maintaining a dry-rated system ideal for indoor environments.

Perfect for modern and traditional spaces alike, the Lark Millie Chandelier brings refined beauty and adaptable functionality to your home.
